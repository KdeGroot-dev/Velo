VELO — SaaS Growth Agency Landing Page
A bold, high-impact agency landing page built for a fictional SaaS growth agency. Designed to demonstrate premium B2B positioning, editorial typography, and conversion-focused layout for the SaaS/tech niche.
Live demo: https://kdegroot-dev.github.io/Velo/

Sections

Hero — Full-viewport headline with oversized editorial type, social proof stats strip (120+ companies, $380M ARR, 94% retention), and dual CTA
Marquee ticker — Scrolling services strip (Product-led Growth · Conversion Optimization · Brand Strategy · Pipeline Acceleration · UX & Web)
Trust bar — Logo row: Notion, Linear, Vercel, Loom, Figma, Stripe, Intercom
Services — 5-card grid: Growth Strategy, Brand & Identity, Web Design & Dev, Demand Generation, Product Marketing
Selected Work — Case study cards with gradient backgrounds and outcome headlines (3× revenue in 8 months, Series A → B in 12 months)
Testimonial — Full-width pull quote with avatar and attribution
Stats ticker — Animated scrolling metrics bar
Numbers — Animated counters: 120+ · $380M · 94%
Process — 3-step: Diagnose → Architect → Accelerate
CTA — "Let's build your future." with primary action button
Footer — Clean 4-link nav with copyright


Built With

HTML5 / CSS3 / Vanilla JavaScript
No frameworks, no templates
Scroll-triggered animations
Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)


Purpose
Portfolio demo piece. Built to show range across SaaS and B2B agency aesthetics — bold, editorial, conversion-focused. Available as a starting point for real agency or SaaS clients.
